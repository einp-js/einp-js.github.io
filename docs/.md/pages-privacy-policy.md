<h1>Privacy Policy</h1>
<p><em>Last updated: May 5, 2026</em></p>
<p>Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how Everything in Perspective collects, uses, and protects your information.</p>
<h2>What information we collect</h2>
<p><strong>When you visit our site:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>IP address, browser type, pages visited (via standard web analytics)</li>
<li>No personal information is collected unless you choose to share it</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>When you subscribe to our newsletter:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Email address (required)</li>
<li>First name (optional)</li>
<li>We use this solely to send you our newsletter and occasional updates</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>When you contact us:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Email address and message content</li>
<li>We keep your message confidential unless you give us permission to share it</li>
</ul>
<h2>How we use your information</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Email subscribers:</strong> Deliver weekly essays and announcements</li>
<li><strong>Analytics:</strong> Understand which topics resonate and improve our content</li>
<li><strong>Contacting you:</strong> Respond to inquiries, topic suggestions, and submissions</li>
</ul>
<p>We do NOT:</p>
<ul>
<li>Sell or share your data with third parties</li>
<li>Use your email for marketing beyond our own newsletter</li>
<li>Track you across other websites</li>
<li>Use dark patterns or manipulative practices</li>
</ul>
<h2>Third-party services</h2>
<p>We use:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Analytics:</strong> Anonymous, aggregated data only</li>
<li><strong>Email service:</strong> Industry-standard encrypted platform</li>
<li><strong>Hosting:</strong> GitHub Pages for secure, private infrastructure</li>
</ul>
<p>All third parties are contractually bound to privacy standards.</p>
<h2>Cookies</h2>
<p>We use minimal cookies:</p>
<ul>
<li>Session cookies for navigation functionality</li>
<li>No tracking cookies for advertisements</li>
<li>No third-party cookies</li>
</ul>
<p>You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting core functionality.</p>
<h2>Your rights</h2>
<p>You have the right to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Access: Request a copy of data we hold about you</li>
<li>Correction: Update or correct your information</li>
<li>Deletion: Request we delete your email from our lists</li>
<li>Opt-out: Unsubscribe from emails at any time</li>
</ul>
<p>To exercise these rights, <a href="/page/contact-us/">contact us</a>.</p>
<h2>Data security</h2>
<p>Your information is protected using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS). Our infrastructure is hosted on secure servers with automatic backups. We regularly audit our security practices.</p>
<h2>Retention</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Newsletter subscribers:</strong> Data retained until you unsubscribe</li>
<li><strong>Contact form submissions:</strong> Retained for 1 year or until resolved</li>
<li><strong>Analytics:</strong> Aggregated data retained indefinitely; personal identifiers deleted after 13 months</li>
</ul>
<h2>Changes to this policy</h2>
<p>We may update this policy as our services evolve. We&#39;ll notify subscribers of material changes via email. Your continued use of our site constitutes acceptance of updated terms.</p>
<h2>Questions?</h2>
<p>Have concerns about your privacy or our practices? <a href="/page/contact-us/">Get in touch</a>. We take privacy seriously and welcome your feedback.</p>
<hr>
<p><em>Everything in Perspective is committed to transparency and ethical data practices. We believe privacy is not a feature—it&#39;s a right.</em></p>
