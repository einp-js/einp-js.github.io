<h1>Frequently Asked Questions</h1>
<h2>What is Everything in Perspective?</h2>
<p>Everything in Perspective is a magazine for readers who want to understand trending topics at depth. We don&#39;t just explain <em>what</em> is happening—we explain <em>why</em> it matters and <em>so what</em> comes next. Each essay combines historical context, multiple perspectives, systemic analysis, and clear writing to cut through noise and provide genuine understanding.</p>
<h2>Who writes for Everything in Perspective?</h2>
<p>Our writers are subject matter experts, researchers, and analysts with deep knowledge across economics, technology, culture, geopolitics, and behavior. Every essay is fact-checked and rigorously analyzed before publication.</p>
<h2>How often do you publish?</h2>
<p>We publish new essays on a regular schedule. New pieces are released when they&#39;re ready, never sacrificing depth for speed. Subscribe to our newsletter to get notified when new essays drop.</p>
<h2>What topics do you cover?</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Economics &amp; Finance</strong> — Markets, policy, and global trade systems</li>
<li><strong>Technology &amp; Society</strong> — Digital transformation and human impact</li>
<li><strong>Culture &amp; Trends</strong> — Ideas, movements, and cultural shifts</li>
<li><strong>Geopolitics &amp; History</strong> — International relations and historical context</li>
<li><strong>Psychology &amp; Behavior</strong> — Human decision-making and collective action</li>
</ul>
<p>We pick topics based on relevance, searchability, and opportunity to provide context that other outlets miss.</p>
<h2>Do you accept submissions or guest essays?</h2>
<p>Yes. If you have expertise on a topic and want to contribute, <a href="/page/contact-us/">contact us</a> with a pitch. We&#39;re looking for rigorous, well-researched essays that fit our editorial standards.</p>
<h2>Can I share your essays?</h2>
<p>Absolutely. Every essay is available to share freely. We encourage readers to distribute our work across social media, newsletters, and with friends. All content is licensed under Creative Commons (CC BY 4.0).</p>
<h2>Do you have a newsletter?</h2>
<p>Yes. Subscribe to get weekly essays delivered to your inbox, along with curated reading recommendations and context on what&#39;s trending.</p>
<h2>How do you make money?</h2>
<p>We&#39;re reader-supported through ethical advertising, subscriptions, and patronage. We don&#39;t sell your data, use manipulative dark patterns, or optimize for engagement over quality. We believe great journalism deserves sustainable funding.</p>
<h2>Why is context important?</h2>
<p>Most content explains the <em>what</em>. News tells you what happened. Social media shows you what&#39;s trending. But without <em>why</em> and <em>so what</em>, you&#39;re left with noise. Context answers:</p>
<ul>
<li>How did we get here historically?</li>
<li>What forces are at play?</li>
<li>Who benefits, who loses?</li>
<li>What happens next?</li>
</ul>
<p>That&#39;s what separates understanding from information overload.</p>
<h2>Can I submit a topic suggestion?</h2>
<p>Yes. Have a topic you think we should cover? <a href="/page/contact-us/">Reach out</a>. We read every suggestion and use reader feedback to guide our editorial calendar.</p>
